Receive and inspect webhooks
Subscribe a callback under Webhooks. Ghost performs hub.challenge verification, signs POST bodies with X-Hub-Signature-256, and retains every event and attempt.

              

              
Use raw request bytes for signature validation. Parse JSON only after comparing the HMAC with your app secret.

            
4
Test templates and the 24-hour window
A customer message opens a sender-and-customer-specific service window for 24 hours. Free-form business messages work inside it; outside it, strict mode returns error 131047 and requires an approved template.

              

              
  1. Open the customer's phone and send the business a first message.
  2. Send free-form API replies and watch sent, delivered, and read ticks.
  3. Use Advance 25h, confirm text is rejected, then send the template.

            
5
Send from multiple business numbers
One business account can contain several sender phones. Templates and webhook subscription belong to the WABA; identity, history, delivery ticks, and customer-service windows belong to each sender/customer pair.
